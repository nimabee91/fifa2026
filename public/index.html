How it works & the rules
  • $5 to enter. Pick any of the 48 teams (more than one person can pick the same team at this stage).
  • If your team is knocked out, you're out — unless you buy back in.
  • Round of 32 buy-back: $10. Pick any team still alive that no surviving player already holds.
  • Round of 16 buy-back: $15. Same rule — and this is the last chance to buy back in.
  • After the Round of 16, all bets are locked. Ride your team to the final.
  • Winner takes all: whoever holds the champion takes the pot. (Split evenly if more than one person holds them.)
  • If nobody picked the champion: the pot goes to whoever's team made it the farthest in the tournament (split evenly on a tie).
  • This site just tracks the pool — settle up with each other (Venmo/cash). The admin marks who's paid.
